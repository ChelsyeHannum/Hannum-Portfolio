IT'S ME, CHELSYE

Before that, in my glory days, I lived in the Netherlands for a year as an Au Pair. Click on the picture of Amsterdam above and you can take a peek at my cringe-worthy blog I wrote during my time there.

I have a dog named Orion (like the constellation, not the lake) and she might as well be my child. She's a terrier mix and runs away just about every week for 15 minutes. Lord help me. She loves everyone and everything and thinks all people are her best friend.

I moved to suburbia for a short time and, although it's not quite the hell I thought it would be, I can't wait to be back in the city. I label myself as "down-to-earth bougie" and these suburbs don't have that down-to-earth feel like Detroit. For now I guess I'll just have to deal with all the chain restaurants and yard work being a go-to conversation.
